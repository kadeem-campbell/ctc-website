About Us

The Caribbean Tech Collective (CTC) is an organisation launched in July 2025 to connect founders, engineers, investors, and professionals of Caribbean background working within technology and technology-adjacent roles.

CTC provides a platform for professional networking, collaboration, and knowledge sharing, with the objective of improving visibility and access to opportunity.

Community Focus

The Caribbean Tech Collective operates as a technology-centred organisation. Programming, discussion, and participation are focused on technology and technology-adjacent work.

CTC prioritises supporting founders, engineers, investors and professionals, placing particular importance on bringing together individuals at similar stages of their professional journey.

Participation Criteria
  • Caribbean background: Individuals of Caribbean descent, whether based in the Caribbean or across the global diaspora.
  • Active building: Founders, investors, or operators actively building companies in the Caribbean.
  • Professional involvement: Individuals working in technology or technology-adjacent fields.
