About

Rhianna Kyeremateng is a data analyst moving into analytics engineering, with a focus on digging into complex problems, collaborating closely with stakeholders, and building trust through well-structured data. Her career blends technical precision with a passion for clarity—whether it’s untangling data pipelines or helping teams make better decisions through insight-led storytelling.

Her wider interests centre on equity and community. Rhianna has been actively involved in initiatives supporting disabled people and the homeless, and she’s deeply invested in amplifying the voices of Black women. For her, community isn’t just a passion—it’s the solution. She believes that community is the answer to many of society’s most pressing challenges, including representation in tech.

As a co-founder of Caribbean Tech Collective, Rhianna leads on content and outreach, using storytelling and strategy to shift narratives. Her vision is to spark cultural pride, deepen the UK-Caribbean connection, and help position the Caribbean as a place of innovation, opportunity, and global relevance. CTC is her way of giving people something to align with—not just culturally, but professionally.
